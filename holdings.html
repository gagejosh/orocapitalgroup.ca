Long-Term Holdings

We hold durable assets with a multi-year horizon. Our emphasis is on resilience, stewardship, and sensible capitalization — with a preference for cash flow and downside protection.

Guiding principles

  • Maintain conservative leverage and ample margin of safety
  • Prioritize asset quality and long-term utility
  • Invest in maintenance and improvements that compound value
  • Think in cycles: plan for volatility, not perfection
