Private Opportunities

We evaluate private opportunities selectively. We prefer simple, well-structured deals where incentives are aligned and the risk profile is understandable.

What we look for

  • Clear alignment between operators, partners, and long-term outcomes
  • Pragmatic structures and transparent reporting
  • A resilient thesis with identifiable downside risks
  • A timeline that supports disciplined execution (not forced exits)
