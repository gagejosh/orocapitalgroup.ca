About

Oro Capital Group is a long-term capital allocator focused on the Columbia Basin and the West Kootenays. We invest with a simple principle: durable outcomes come from disciplined underwriting, patient timelines, and alignment with the communities where capital is deployed.

What we believe

  • Long horizons: we prioritize multi-year outcomes over short-term optimization.
  • Fundamentals first: cash flow, downside protection, and simple structures matter.
  • Local stewardship: we value relationships, reputation, and responsible execution.
  • Selectivity: we prefer fewer opportunities with clear theses and sensible risk.

What we focus on

Our work spans development projects, private opportunities, and long-term holdings. The unifying theme is thoughtful capital allocation — choosing opportunities where careful structure and execution can compound over time.

Where we operate

We are based in British Columbia with a local focus in the West Kootenays and the broader Columbia Basin. We aim to contribute to resilient local growth through pragmatic, well-executed projects.
